=== Dunbar Social ===
Contributors: Ben Cavenagh
Tested up to: 5.5
Stable tag: 1.5

Dunbar Social's first theme. Pre-launch, November 2020.

== Description ==

The year 2020 has been wild. Kicking off with Australia becoming a fiery hellscape, Kobe dying, and rumors of Kim Jong Un's death
to a global pandemic during an election year. Toilet paper supplies hit an all time low and boredom an all time high. To become a 
hero all you really needed to do was sit on a couch. But one thing emerged from the ashes. One beacon of hope to be delivered in 2021.
A vision of a better future, we present to you Dunbar.
